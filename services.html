Common questions

FAQs.

Do you operate in my postcode or area?

We serve Essex, Hertfordshire, Cambridgeshire, London and Suffolk.

Do you operate in the evening and at weekends?

Yes — our locksmiths are on call 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Will your locksmith have the right parts on the van?

Our locksmiths carry a comprehensive range of locks and associated security parts on well-stocked vans. If we don't carry your lock, we never charge to leave site to go and purchase it, unlike most of our competitors.

Can you open, repair or change locks?

We can open most locks non-destructively, and aim to repair rather than replace locks, to reduce your costs.

What's the difference between a 3 lever and a 5 lever lock?

A 3 lever lock is less secure because it has fewer levers, which increases the risk of key duplication. A 3 lever lock will NOT be BS3621 approved due to its poor security.

What guarantee do I get?

All parts are guaranteed for 12 months, and we always provide a receipt on completion of work.

How can I pay?

Credit and debit cards, cash, or BACS.

I've snapped my key in the lock — should I remove it myself?

No — attempting to remove a broken key yourself is probably the worst thing you can do, as it can damage the lock's internal components and push the broken piece further in, making extraction more time-consuming and costly. Call us instead.

Can you gain entry to a friend's or relative's property?

No, we can only gain entry with the owner's express permission.
