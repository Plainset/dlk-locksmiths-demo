About DLK Locksmiths

A family business since 1999.

Founded in 1999 by Darren and Lisa, DLK Locksmiths is a local, family-run and family-owned 24-hour emergency locksmith service, offering an honest, reliable and comprehensive service for both domestic and commercial customers at competitive prices.

All of our locksmiths are Essex & Met Police registered. With over 20 years' experience in locks and security, we handle security installations, non-destructive emergency lock opening, burglary repairs, insurance surveys and burglary prevention advice — on call 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.
